Our Leadership

Annette Muma Chota

Founder & Lead Practitioner

Experienced Legal Advocate with Deep Institutional Insight

Annette Muma Chota is a distinguished legal practitioner with nearly a decade of experience in the Zambian legal landscape. Her professional journey includes key roles such as Corporate Legal Intern at Airtel Networks Zambia, Legal Officer at the National Legal Aid Clinic for Women, and Research Advocate for the Judiciary of the Republic of Zambia.

Her diverse background across corporate, non-profit, and judicial sectors empowers her to provide comprehensive legal solutions with unique strategic insight. She is committed to delivering excellence and ensuring that every client receives personalized, effective legal representation.
